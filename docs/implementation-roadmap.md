# Implementation Roadmap

## Phase 1 — Core MVP (3–4 months)
- Authentication, RBAC, staff profiles.
- CRM (clients/pets, base medical records).
- Scheduling calendar + reminders.
- Invoice/payment baseline + Slovak VAT/eKasa integration.
- Dashboard v1.

## Phase 2 — Clinical Tooling (2–3 months)
- AI transcription ingestion pipeline.
- Encounter notes drafts + approval workflow.
- Lab/imaging imports + document store.
- Medication prescribing baseline.

## Phase 3 — Automation (2–3 months)
- AI extraction into structured fields.
- Auto invoice drafts from encounters.
- Automatic inventory deduction on invoice approval.
- Smart alerts and rules engine.

## Phase 4 — E-Commerce (2 months)
- Product catalog, filtering, cart, checkout.
- Order management, tracking, returns.
- Loyalty points and recommendation hooks.

## Phase 5 — Advanced AI & Communication (3–4 months)
- After-hours AI phone assistant.
- Newsletter automation and segmentation.
- Telemedicine + omnichannel messaging.
- No-show/churn predictive models.

## Phase 6 — Optimization & BI (2 months)
- Advanced analytics suite and forecasting.
- Query/performance hardening.
- Staff mobile workflows.

## Phase 7 — Compliance Hardening (1–2 months)
- Controlled substance workflows.
- Data governance, retention, export/delete automation.
- Security audits, penetration testing, operational readiness.
